Dr. Chuck Tribute Page

Charles is a Clinical Professor and teaches in the School of Information at the University of Michigan.
He teaches over popular Massively Open Online Courses (MOOCs) including
Python for Everybody - the most popular online programming course in the world on the Coursera,
edX, and FutureLearn platforms. He is also a long-time advocate of open source educational technology and
open educational resources to empower teachers.
